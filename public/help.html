How to create a card?


To create a card go to games page by clicking on the button on the top of the page named "Games", in there you choose witch template you would like to create a card with by clicking on one of them. After you will be redirected to the creation page, on that page use the buttons and text areas to custom your card as you like.

How to download?


After you finish customizing your card just scroll down the creation page a little on the bottom of the card you will find a button that asks what you want to do with the card, click on that button and select the option "Download card", and them click on the button that will appear right down and the card will be downloaded.

How to save?


After you finish customizing your card just scroll down the creation page a little on the bottom of the card you will find a button that asks what you want to do with the card, click on that button and select the option "Save card" and them click on the button that will appear right down and the card will be saved to your profile.

Note: you can only save cards if you have an account!!!
