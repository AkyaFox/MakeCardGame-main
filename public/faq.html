  • Why do you create the website?

    This site is an academic work where we can develop anything as long as the methodologies used are in accordance with the curriculum of the course, so we took this information and made a card game creator, suggested by our advisor and embraced by us.

  • Why the choice of theme was creating card games?

    On the internet I've found several board game sites online and of course we also found many card creation sites, but one point we realized is that many of these sites had limited systems, so I decided to create my own based on what I was expecting from other systems.

  • You are Brazilian, why a site in English?

    I decided to develop the site in English as it is technically a universal language, everyone should know English nowadays, so the site will have a larger audience of people.
